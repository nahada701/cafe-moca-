Menu


Espresso

Made by diluting a shot of espresso with hot water, resulting in a milder flavor compared to espresso. It resembles traditional brewed coffee but with a slightly different taste.

Americano

A concentrated shot of coffee brewed by forcing hot water through finely ground coffee beans. It has a rich, intense flavor and is the foundation for many other coffee drinks..

Cappuccino

Equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam. This classic Italian coffee has a balanced flavor and a layer of velvety foam on top..

Latte

Made with espresso and steamed milk, topped with a small amount of milk foam. It has a smooth and creamy texture, with a mild coffee flavor.

Macchiato

A shot of espresso "marked" with a dollop of milk foam on top. It offers a strong coffee taste with a touch of creaminess.

Mocha

A combination of espresso, steamed milk, chocolate syrup, and topped with whipped cream. It offers a delightful blend of coffee and chocolate flavors..
